Challenge

🏢 At a company as large as Bank of America, over 10,000 applications generate countless projects and tickets.

🔍 With such volume, it is nearly impossible for any individual or team to fully understand the scope of ongoing work.

♻️ As a result, significant effort is often spent on redundant projects that have already been completed.

Solution

🤖 Developed a model to help users quickly determine if their current work has been previously completed.

📝 Users enter a brief description of their work, and the system ranks similar projects and tickets by relevance.

📬 Users can then review details and identify the most relevant matches and key contacts.

My Role

💡 Proposed and led the development of a new model after prior work with GBM models.

🛠️ Built the model in RStudio using Quanteda, and created an interactive frontend with R Shiny.

🤝 Collaborated with PMO officials to validate the system's accuracy in identifying similar efforts.
