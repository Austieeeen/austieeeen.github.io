Challenge

📅 Every month, 100 domains were required to submit 3–4 metrics on data accuracy and timeliness.

📝 This process, which involved calculating and emailing results, was slow, error-prone, and inconsistent.

⏳ Ultimately, it consumed 3,000 labor hours per year and resulted in approximately $4.5 million in lost productivity.

Solution

🚀 To address this challenge, we launched an initiative to develop a custom software solution tailored to our needs.

💻 Our team engineered a full-stack application that enables clients to automatically calculate and submit metrics to a centralized platform.

📊 This new system provides a one-stop shop for reporting and metric management—reducing labor hours, enhancing data quality with built-in guardrails, and streamlining workflows for all users.

My Role

🔎 As the only employee with full visibility into the broken process and the means to address it, I championed the initiative to drive change.

🤝 I brought together stakeholders and technology resources, architected the solution, and delegated development to our tech partners.

📈 Lastly, I managed the project through completion and worked directly with clients to onboard them to the new platform.
