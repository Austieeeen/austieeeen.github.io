About

My name is Austin and I was born in raised in Jacksonville, NC. 20 minutes away from the beach, my mind was more focused on sitting in the sand than programming my day away. After graduating from Jacksonville High School (Go Cardinals!) in 2018, I fell in love with computer science in my first year at NC State.

After coming into college an Aerospace Engineering major, learning a language as simple as MatLab was the catalyst for a major switch; and I never looked back. Now, three years down the line and with a year of experience under my belt, I've learned more than I ever thought possible and plan to continue to do so in the future.
